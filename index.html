Scientific integration consultancy

Clowder Works

When collaboration needs to produce a coherent scientific whole, Clowder Works provides the dedicated integration function that ambitious interdisciplinary projects need — aligning concepts, methods, data, and stakeholder perspectives across disciplines and work packages.

What we do Get in touch

Why integration matters

%

The gap that project management doesn't fill

Integration matters because it helps a collaboration produce more than a collection of separate contributions. It creates shared understanding across disciplines and connects different kinds of expertise.

Even more, it turns diversity in methods, data, and perspectives into new scientific value. That makes projects more coherent, proposals more credible, and outcomes more relevant to research and practice.

It also gives Principal Investigators the space to focus on scientific leadership, while integration is handled as a recognised part of the work.

"Integration is a separate intellectual task — not a coordination problem, but a scientific one."

What we do

Three ways Clowder Works supports your project

01 — Proposal development

Design the integration logic before reviewers ask for it

For Horizon Europe and national collaborative calls, Clowder Works helps design how disciplines connect, where stakeholder input enters, how knowledge will be translated across domains, and how integration is built into work packages rather than left implicit. Complex consortia become easier for reviewers to assess — and more credible.

02 — Implementation support

Structured alignment through the life of the project

Facilitated workshops, communication structures, and reflection formats that surface misalignments early — before they become delays, duplication, or scientifically incompatible outputs. Support for alignment across work packages, disciplines, and stakeholder groups.

03 — Training & capacity building

Institutional capacity for interdisciplinary research

Training for universities, doctoral schools, and project teams in interdisciplinary and transdisciplinary collaboration, facilitation, integration frameworks, and stakeholder engagement — building lasting capacity, not generic team development.

How to engage

Collaboration pathways

01

Institutional programmes

Workshops and structured training for university faculties, medical schools, doctoral schools, and PhD networks. Designed for research leadership seeking to build integration capacity across their institution rather than project by project.

02

Team-level collaboration

Tailored, in-depth work with individual research teams: integration design, facilitated alignment, and structured support through proposal development or project implementation.

03

Proposal partnership

Embedded collaboration on a specific funding application — co-developing the integration logic, work package structure, and stakeholder mapping for Horizon Europe or national calls.

Research integration

What is research integration

Integration turns separate expertise into something new.

It brings concepts, methods, data, and stakeholder knowledge into one coherent research design.

It helps people across fields understand each other, work through mismatches, and build a shared way of thinking.

The result is research that no single discipline could produce alone.

A project manager keeps the project moving. An integrator makes the science work together.
