Lead expert & founder

Ekaterina Kutafina

Scientific Integration Specialist

Ekaterina holds doctorates in applied mathematics and theoretical medicine. For more than a decade she has worked at the intersection of computational medicine, medical data science, and research infrastructure — combining active research, research leadership, and direct experience with clinical data systems.

She is involved in meta-research on inter- and transdisciplinarity as a member of the Global Alliance on Inter- and Transdisciplinary Research, which informs how Clowder Works approaches integration methodology.

This background positions her to work at peer level with both scientific and technical partners — contributing to integration design as a practitioner, not as a process consultant.
