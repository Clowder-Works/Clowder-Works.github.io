Ekaterina Kutafina

Scientific Integration Specialist

Ekaterina Kutafina holds doctorates in applied mathematics and theoretical medicine and has worked for more than a decade at the intersection of computational medicine, medical data science, and research infrastructure. Her background includes active research, research leadership, and experience with clinical data systems and research infrastructure initiatives, which positions her to work at peer level with both scientific and technical partners in complex collaborations. She is also involved in meta-research on inter- and transdisciplinarity as a member of the Global Alliance on Inter- and Transdisciplinary Research.
